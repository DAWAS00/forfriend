Royalties

Royalties are payments made to the owner of a particular asset
(usually intellectual property or natural resources) for the ongoing use of that asset.
They are often calculated as a percentage of revenue or profit generated from using the asset.

Our Mission

Royalties is to empower creators, innovators, and rights holders by ensuring they receive fair
and transparent compensation for the use of their intellectual property. We aim to build trust, drive value,
and support sustainable growth through reliable royalty management solutions.

How We Help

To train new employees on the correct procedure for booking royalties by providing a practical and interactive
guide that simplifies the process, saves time, and promotes self-reliance, reducing the need for direct supervisor involvement.
