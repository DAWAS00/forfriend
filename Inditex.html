How to calculate Inditex royalties:

  • We extract gross sales from JDE for the period required and check if it equals the sales in the invoice 
  • We multiply the sales with the royalty provision as per the agreement 
  • We calculate the difference between the result and the provision 
  • We add the business unit on BPM for the brand and it depends if it is online or offline sales 
  • We extract the TB as per the below: 

    Fill Fiscal Year
    Period-From
    Period-To
  • After checking the sales: We take the provision divided by the JDE exchange rate , the remaining balance to be booked on DOE (difference in exchange rate ) How do we check that the amount booked on DOE is correct:
  • provision amount / rate on JDE
  • provision / (1/rate on the invoice)
  • the difference is the DOE to be booked on 9523 or 9524
